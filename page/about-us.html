About us

This is the official website of SIPL.

SIPL is the Signal and Image Processing Lab located at Shiraz University. This lab was established in 2015 in order to encourage the research spirit in students. Here at SIPL, we look for talented and innovative undergraduate and graduate students that want a friendly and encouraging environment to conduct their research and carry out their projects.
